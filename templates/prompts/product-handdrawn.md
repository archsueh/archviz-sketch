# 产品手绘 Prompt Template (for archviz-sketch)

**Trigger:** 产品手绘, 造型基础, 爆炸图, 剖切

**Base Prompt:**

Create a 2K landscape product design sketch for "{产品/对象}".

Style: Industrial design process hand sketch on white sketch paper.
Mode: {3/4 perspective / exploded view / cutaway section / ideation sheet with multiple angles}

Tools: Pencil construction lines + black fineliner + light gray marker for shadows. Visible ellipses and construction guides.

Annotations: {功能/尺寸/材料标注词列表} in neat but hand-written style.

Key requirements:
- Clear exploded or cutaway to show internal structure if relevant
- Natural perspective with slight imperfection
- Construction lines and guide marks visible
- Professional yet "in progress" design energy
- Generous margins and breathing room

Negative prompt:
Photorealistic render, glossy 3D, perfect CAD lines, heavy shading, no construction lines, vector clean look.

**Sub-types:**
- Exploded view: separate components with alignment arrows and labels
- Cutaway: section plane with hatching on cut surfaces
- Ideation sheet: 3-4 quick variants side by side with quick notes

**Quality Checklist:**
- Construction geometry visible
- Materials suggested through line weight (not color fills)
- Annotations integrated without clutter
- Perspective feels believable

**400-free reference:**
Draw inspiration from "Lukasz Adam" product sketches and "DrawKit" for clean exploded logic.
