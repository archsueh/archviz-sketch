# 瑞士网格与维涅里标识 (Swiss Modernist / Vignelli Grid) Prompt Template

**Trigger:** 瑞士网格, Swiss grid, Vignelli canon, minimal print, flat poster

**Base Prompt:**

Create a high-resolution 2K image of "{{subject}}" in a strict Swiss Modernist poster style.

Visual DNA:
- Off-white paper background (#f4f1ea) or pure white.
- Pure flat graphic design. No 3D shadows, no textures, no gradients, no photorealism.
- Strict modular grid structure. Column alignment is highly visible and precise.
- Large, bold sans-serif display lettering (Helvetica or Haas Grotesque feel) flush-left, ragged-right. Never justify text.
- Maximum contrast of scale: very large title text vs very small labels.
- Hairline rules and rulers (0.5–2pt) acting as strict structural anchors for typography and shapes.
- Accent color: A single bright identifier (e.g. Swiss Red #e4002b or Vermilion #f04e23), used selectively to denote structure or flow. Max 1 accent.

Composition:
- Clear typographic hierarchy.
- Bold graphic shapes aligned to grid lines.
- Asymmetrical balance held in tension.
- Generous empty white space (at least 45%).

Negative prompt:
3D effects, drop shadows, gradients, realistic textures, cluttered design, decorative elements, serif fonts, justified text, multiple accent colors.

**Quality Checklist:**
- Layout adheres to a visible vertical and horizontal grid.
- Type is flush-left and aligned perfectly.
- Colors are extremely restrained (1 primary accent max).
- Space feels silent and structured.
